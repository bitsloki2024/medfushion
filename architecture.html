② Data Pipeline — How a Request Flows
Step 1 · Ingest
External APIs
Disease.sh · WHO GHO · CDC · ECDC · MMWR RSS · HealthMap · IHME CSV
Step 2 · Fetch & Parse
data_sources/
Each module fetches its source · Parses REST / OData / RSS / CSV · Normalises to standard schema
Step 3 · Cache
cache/ (TTL)
Saved to JSON on disk · 1 hr (live sources) · 24 hr (static) · Demo works offline
Step 4 · Analyse
ml/ models
IsolationForest anomaly detection · Prophet forecast · KMeans risk classification
Step 5 · Serve
FastAPI Routes
/globe/heatmap · /country/stats · /forecast · /alerts · /risk/score — JSON responses
Step 6 · Render
Next.js Frontend
fetch() from page.tsx · Globe dots update · Country panel populates · Stats recalculate
Step 7 · Display
User sees live data
3D globe · Risk scores · Trend charts · Anomaly flags · Forecasts · Outbreak alerts
